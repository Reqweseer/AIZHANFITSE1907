описание

Kayden is a mysterious man who, like a comet, descended from the sky. All wounded, almost losing his last strength, he decides to hide his presence - after all, there is no other way to escape from the chase. On the way, Kayden meets an ordinary yard cat and, almost without thinking, decides to move into his body. Seo Ji-woo is an energetic and sociable young man from high school who loves cats very much. Once on the way home, he meets a painfully battered cat and takes it to his home. Our So Ji Woo is not only kind, but also has one hidden ability. What is it?
